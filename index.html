Welcome to Traverse!

Traverse is tool for Star Citizen that helps you to Traverse the Verse. Traverse does so with a set of features that helps you explore the verse.

Features

Verse Starmap - A tree hierarchy showing locations in Star Citizen.
Shopping and Trading - Buy/Sell prices of items and commodities at their locations and price comparisons.
Search - Search everything for whatever you're looking for.
Popular Searches - A list of common and helpful searches made by players.
